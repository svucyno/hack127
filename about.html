How It Works
From order to delivery — a complete retail workflow.
1
Customer Places Order
Browse products, add to cart, checkout. Order status: New.
2
Staff Accepts & Packs
Admin/Cashier sees the order, accepts it, packs items. Status: Packing.
3
Bill Generated Automatically
Packing done triggers auto bill generation with GST breakdown. Status: Ready.
4
Customer Picks Up & Confirms
Customer comes to store, takes items, confirms receipt. Status: Completed. Analytics update.
